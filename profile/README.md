## Overview

<a href="#">Loom</a> is a video messaging platform for Mac that replaces lengthy written explanations and unnecessary meetings with short, clear screen recordings. The application captures your screen, webcam, or both simultaneously, producing an instantly shareable video link the moment recording stops. Recipients watch at their own pace, eliminating the scheduling friction that synchronous communication requires while preserving the clarity that text alone cannot deliver.

The <a href="#">recording experience</a> on Mac is designed for speed. A click on the menu bar icon launches the recorder with camera bubble, microphone, and screen region already configured from previous sessions. Record the full screen, a specific window, or a custom-drawn region. The <a href="#">camera bubble</a> overlays your face in a movable circle, adding personal presence to walkthroughs and explanations without requiring a separate video call setup. Recording begins in seconds and stops just as quickly, producing a video that uploads automatically in the background while you return to work.

<a href="#">Instant sharing</a> is Loom's defining workflow advantage. As soon as recording ends, a link is copied to the clipboard ready to paste into Slack, email, Notion, Jira, or any other tool your team uses. Recipients click the link and watch immediately — no account required, no file download, no waiting for a large attachment to transfer. <a href="#">Viewer reactions and comments</a> appear timestamped on the video timeline, letting collaborators respond to specific moments rather than writing vague references to what they saw.

<a href="#">Workspace organization</a> keeps videos accessible across teams. Folders group recordings by project, team, or workflow. Shared workspaces give colleagues access to relevant libraries without manually forwarding individual links. <a href="#">Video privacy controls</a> restrict viewing to specific email addresses or require a password, ensuring sensitive screen recordings stay within the intended audience.

## Key Features

- <a href="#">One-click screen recording</a> captures full screen window or custom region with camera overlay and microphone simultaneously
- <a href="#">Instant shareable link</a> copies to clipboard the moment recording ends ready to paste anywhere without upload delays
- <a href="#">Camera bubble overlay</a> displays your face as a movable circle on the recording for personal walkthroughs and explanations
- <a href="#">Timestamped viewer comments</a> let recipients respond to specific moments in the video timeline with reactions and text
- <a href="#">Automatic transcription</a> generates searchable text from video audio enabling content discovery across the workspace library
- <a href="#">AI-powered video summaries</a> condense recordings into key points so viewers can decide whether to watch the full video
- <a href="#">Team workspace organization</a> groups videos in shared folders accessible to colleagues without manual link distribution
- <a href="#">Privacy and access controls</a> restrict viewing by email domain password or link-only access for sensitive recordings
- <a href="#">Slack and Notion integration</a> embeds Loom videos directly in messages and documents with inline preview playback

## Additional Information

Loom addresses the specific communication overhead that remote and distributed teams experience daily. Written explanations of complex processes lose nuance. Scheduled video calls require coordination across time zones. Loom sits between these two extremes — more expressive than text, more flexible than a synchronous call — making it a practical fit for engineering teams doing code reviews, designers sharing mockup walkthroughs, and managers delivering detailed feedback.

The Mac application runs as a lightweight menu bar tool that stays out of the way until needed. Recording quality remains high without requiring professional equipment or setup time. For teams that have adopted async-first communication, Loom provides the video layer that makes asynchronous work genuinely effective.
